Frictionless payments with PayPal. PayPal Digital Goods is the only one-click PayPal payment solution.

== Description ==

Give shoppers on your WooCommerce store a frictionless checkout, with PayPal Digital Goods.

PayPal Digital Goods is PayPal's only one-click payment solution. It includes in-context payment so buyers don't leave your website to make a payment. Once a buyer has purchased an item on your site, they no longer need to log in to PayPal for future purchases.

Learn more about [PayPal Digital Goods >>](https://merchant.paypal.com/cgi-bin/marketingweb?cmd=_render-content&content_ID=merchant/digital_goods)

== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire `/woocommerce-gateway-paypal-digital-goods/` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin.
1. Navigate to **PayPal Digital Goods* section of the **Payment Gateways** tab on the *WooCommerce > Settings* page. For a site on `example.com`, this page would be found by clicking **PayPal Digital Goods** at `http://example.com/wp-admin/admin.php?page=woocommerce&tab=payment_gateways`.
1. Set your API credentials and configure the other settings.

If you do no have your Paypal API credentials, learn how to obtain them [here](http://docs.woothemes.com/document/paypal-digital-goods-for-express-checkout-gateway/#step3obtainingyourpaypalapicredentials).

If you do not have a PayPal Business account configured to use PayPay Digital Goods for Express Checkout, you can learn how to configure your account [here](http://docs.woothemes.com/document/paypal-digital-goods-for-express-checkout-gateway/).
